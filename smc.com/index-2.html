About Us

Gulf Health Recruit was created, when a group of professionals took upon the shortage of competent doctors, faced by health-care Industry. An Industry solution was the need of the hour, and we are glad we did it.

Our response to queries have been rapid without affecting the quality of results, as a system driven approach to Candidates ensures timely addressing to placement demands, be it interview arrangements, document clearance, candidate on-boarding.

On the other hand, we listen to our clients and adapt to their situation and assist them in being able to fill very specific needs. We get to know our customers well. All of this is due to the successful strategy and leadership style of management shown by our team in Dubai. From the selection of the staff to the daily follow-up and support that your candidates receive.

At GHR, we believe that hiring and retaining exceptional quality staff is paramount to an organization's success. Partnering the right individual with a business’s needs and objectives is crucial. We have an extensive database of high calibre candidates from all grades and specialities with whom we have established relationships and who choose to work with us exclusively.

VISION

The focus of Gulf Health Recruit is to become the leading Consulting Company in Healthcare Industry in U.A.E.

MISSION

Gulf Health Recruit pursues the highest goals in the sector through:

  • Excellence in healthcare sourcing.
  • Ensuring accurate advice to our candidates on credentialing and placement opportunities.
  • Ensure immediate response to Customer & Candidate Queries. Acting as a responsible corporate citizen.
